About

More than 10 years of commercial cleaning in Far North Queensland.

MCDS is a locally owned and operated cleaning business specialising in office spaces, clinical rooms, and medical facilities.

Our work includes office deep cleaning, medical facility cleaning, eco-friendly cleaning, and detailed window and floor care, with services shaped around the way each site actually runs day to day.

The focus is simple: professionalism, patient confidentiality, consistency, and a workplace that feels pristine, sanitised, and properly looked after without disrupting your team or your clients.
